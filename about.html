What is Sachivalayam?

Sachivalayam is a revolutionary initiative by the Government of Andhra Pradesh that brings government services directly to the citizens at the ward/secretariat level. Each Sachivalayam serves approximately 2,000 to 3,000 households, ensuring that no citizen has to travel long distances to access essential services.

Established under the village/ward secretariat system, Sachivalayams function as one-stop service centers where trained staff assist citizens with certificates, welfare schemes, pensions, ration cards, and grievance redressal — all under one roof.

With digital integration and dedicated staff, Sachivalayams aim to eliminate middlemen, reduce corruption, and provide transparent, time-bound delivery of services to every citizen regardless of their location or economic status.

Our Objectives

Key goals that drive the Sachivalayam initiative forward.

🎯 Decentralized Governance

Bring government services closer to citizens by establishing ward-level secretariats in every village and urban ward across the state.

⚡ Fast Service Delivery

Ensure time-bound processing of applications with clear timelines and accountability at every step of the service delivery chain.

🔍 Transparency

Eliminate intermediaries and provide direct access to government services with full transparency in processes and fee structures.

📱 Digital Integration

Leverage technology for online applications, status tracking, and digital record-keeping to improve efficiency and reduce paperwork.

🤝 Inclusive Access

Ensure that elderly, disabled, and marginalized citizens receive equal access to all welfare schemes and government services.

📊 Data-Driven Planning

Maintain accurate citizen databases at ward level for effective planning and targeted delivery of welfare programs.

Benefits to Citizens

How Sachivalayam makes a difference in your daily life.

✓No need to visit district offices — services available at ward level
✓Dedicated staff assigned to assist with applications and queries
✓Free or nominal fee services with no hidden charges
✓Grievance redressal mechanism for unresolved issues
✓Regular outreach camps for pension and scheme enrollment
✓Digital tracking of application status
✓Special assistance for senior citizens and persons with disabilities
✓Timely disbursement of pensions and welfare benefits
