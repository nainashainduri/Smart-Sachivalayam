Citizen Services

Complete guide to services available at your Sachivalayam

Home / Services
📜

Certificates Information

Apply for various official certificates required for education, employment, and government schemes.

  • Birth Certificate — Required documents: Hospital record, parent Aadhaar
  • Death Certificate — Required documents: Hospital/death report, applicant ID
  • Marriage Certificate — Required documents: Marriage registration, witness details
  • Processing time: 7–15 working days depending on certificate type
  • Visit Sachivalayam with original documents and photocopies
👴

Pension Services

Social security pensions for eligible citizens including old age, widow, and disability pensions.

  • Old Age Pension — Age 60+ with BPL status, ₹2,000/month
  • Widow Pension — For widows above 18 years, income criteria apply
  • Disability Pension — 40%+ disability with medical certificate
  • Disbursement on 1st and 15th of every month
  • Annual life certificate verification required
🛒

Ration Card Services

Apply for new ration cards, modifications, and member additions/deletions.

  • New Ration Card — Aadhaar of all members, address proof, passport photo
  • Member Addition — Birth certificate or Aadhaar of new member
  • Category Change — Income certificate for APL/BPL conversion
  • Duplicate Card — FIR copy in case of lost card
  • Biometric verification mandatory for all cardholders
💰

Income Certificate

Required for scholarships, fee concessions, and welfare scheme eligibility.

  • Valid for 1 year from date of issue
  • Required: Aadhaar, address proof, self-declaration of income
  • Processing time: 7 working days
  • Fee: Free of cost at Sachivalayam
📋

Caste Certificate

For reservation benefits in education and employment.

  • Required: Parent's caste certificate or school records
  • Affidavit and community certificate from local authority
  • Processing time: 15 working days
  • Verification by Revenue official may be required
🏠

Residence Certificate

Proof of residence for domicile and local quota purposes.

  • Required: Aadhaar, voter ID, or utility bill as address proof
  • Minimum 5 years residence in the ward for domicile certificate
  • Processing time: 7 working days
  • Valid for 3 years
📢

Grievance Services

Register and track complaints related to any Sachivalayam service.

  • Register grievances in person or through contact form
  • Receive acknowledgment with tracking number
  • Resolution timeline: 15–30 days depending on issue
  • Escalation to Mandal level if unresolved within 30 days
  • Monthly grievance redressal camps held at Sachivalayam
